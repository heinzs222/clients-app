Setup Guide

To configure your endpoint, you need two pieces of information from your Meta Business Manager:

  1. Go to Data Sources in Events Manager.
  2. Select your Pixel (Dataset).
  3. Go to the Settings tab.
  4. Scroll down to find the Dataset ID.
  5. Further down, under Conversions API, click Generate access token.
